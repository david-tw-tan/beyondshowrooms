A Smarter Way to Furnish

Whether it's trusting your taste, fearing costly mistakes, or not knowing where to start, we're here to make furnishing large homes easier.

Beyond Showrooms starts you off with curated design directions, inspired by luxury brands and design-magazine interiors.

You choose a direction. We tailor it to your needs and budget. Then we deliver it end-to-end, factory-direct from our network of premium manufacturers in Foshan, China's furniture capital.

The result: a home you'll love, not just tolerate. You'll furnish with more confidence and less stress — with us beside you every step of the way.
