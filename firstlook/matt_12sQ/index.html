What Happens Next?

  1. You review this First Look

    Note what resonates and what doesn't — even a rough reaction is enough to work with.

  2. We get on a call

    A relaxed 20–30 minute conversation to go through your feedback. Ask us anything about our process — and we'll clarify timing, budget, and any key concerns. By the end, we'll both know if we're a fit. If yes, let's proceed to Step 3.

  3. We build out your full collection

    Once we're aligned on style, we put together a complete room-by-room selection — customized to your home, budget, and needs.

  4. You decide whether to place an order

    No pressure, no minimums — we work at your pace. Ready to move forward? Tell us whether you'd rather order remotely or visit Foshan first. Either way, we've got you covered at every step.
